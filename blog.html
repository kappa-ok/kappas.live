Room For Two, In Depth.

The image over on the left is the self-titled peice Room For Two. The piece was created in Krita, which is my application of choice of digital art. The piece uses a style influenced by Keith Haring and a few other cartoonists. I made it as a test of a new brush type I downloaded, it's very simply about being kind and giving hope to the people around you. I think it's a pretty nice looking piece.

Weekly Music Recommendations

  • Modest Mouse

    • Modest Mouse is a Washington based indie project that combines a variety of genres into a unique blend that has brought the band to a level of mainstream success that most indie bands have yet to see.

  • Car Seat Headrest

    • Car Seat Headrest is an indie band based in seattle. They combine interesting and intimate lyricism with powerful and percise drum rhythms and soaring guitar melodies to create a sound worthy of attention.

  • German Error Message

    • German Error Message is a folk project from Nashville based musician Paul Kintzing. The sound is reminiscent of a haunted barn.
